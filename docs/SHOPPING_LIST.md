## Core Electronics ($80-120)

### Microcontroller
- **ESP32 Development Board** - $15-20
  - Alternative: Arduino UNO + WiFi Module ($25-30)
  - Where to buy: Amazon, AliExpress, Local electronics store
  - Look for: ESP32 DevKit V1, 30-pin version

### Sensors  
- **Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor** - $5-8
  - Brand: YL-69 or similar analog sensor
  - **Important**: Get capacitive (corrosion resistant), NOT resistive
  - Where: Amazon, electronics suppliers

- **DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensor** - $8-12
  - Alternative: DHT11 (cheaper but less accurate)
  - Includes: Sensor + 4-pin connector
  - Where: Amazon, Adafruit, SparkFun

### Power & Control
- **5V Single Channel Relay Module** - $3-5
  - Rating: 10A @ 250VAC minimum
  - Features: LED indicator, optocoupler isolation
  - Where: Amazon, electronics stores

- **12V 2A Power Supply** - $10-15
  - For water pump operation
  - Wall adapter with DC connector
  - Where: Electronics stores, Amazon

## Water System Components ($30-60)

### Pump & Water Delivery
- **12V DC Water Pump** - $15-25
  - Submersible or inline pump
  - Flow rate: 2-5 L/min
  - Head: 2-3 meters
  - Where: Amazon, aquarium stores

- **Silicone Tubing (Food Grade)** - $8-12
  - Inner diameter: 6-8mm
  - Length: 5-10 meters
  - Material: Food grade silicone
  - Where: Amazon, brewing supply stores

- **Drip Irrigation Kit** - $10-20
  - Adjustable drippers (2-8 L/hour)
  - T-connectors and fittings
  - Stakes for plant positioning
  - Where: Garden centers, Amazon

## Money-Saving Tips

### Budget Optimization
1. **Start Small**: Begin with ESP32 + basic sensors
2. **Generic Components**: Use compatible alternatives
3. **Bulk Buying**: Order multiple sensors for future projects
4. **Local Sourcing**: Check local electronics stores
5. **Used Equipment**: Raspberry Pi and basic hardware

### DIY Alternatives
- **Water Reservoir**: Repurpose large plastic containers
- **Enclosure**: Use food containers with modifications
- **Tubing**: Aquarium airline tubing (smaller diameter)
- **Mounting**: 3D print custom brackets

## Quality vs Cost Guidelines

### Don't Compromise On:
- **ESP32/Arduino**: Reliable microcontroller is essential
- **Power Supply**: Stable 12V supply for pump operation
- **Waterproofing**: Protect electronics from moisture
- **Soil Sensor**: Capacitive sensor for longevity

### Can Save Money On:
- **Enclosure**: DIY waterproof housing
- **Wiring**: Generic jumper wires
- **Accessories**: LEDs, resistors, connectors
- **Server**: Use existing computer instead of Pi
